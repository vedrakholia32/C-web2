The academic programmes are recognized by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and college is affiliated to Gujarat Technological University

Vision

To develop competent professionals with excellent technical skills, research aptitude and core values.

Mission

To create student and faculty centric culture that imparts technical education.
To provide conducive environment for innovation, creativity and research to students and faculties.
To inculcate core values through various technical events and cultural programs.
